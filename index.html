Complete Guide ‚Äì National Vocational Qualifications Sri Lanka

REF_CODE: 101-F-NVQ / 102-F-NVQ

‚úÖ What is NVQ?

NVQ (National Vocational Qualification) is a skills-based qualification framework in Sri Lanka that is nationally recognized by TVEC and internationally recognized in 180+ countries. You can start NVQ even if you failed O/Levels!

üåü Key Advantages

  • ‚úì Can start without O/L or A/L passes (lower levels)
  • ‚úì Learn practical skills immediately applicable to jobs
  • ‚úì Earn while learning (apprenticeship modes)
  • ‚úì International portability (work anywhere)
  • ‚úì Faster path to employment (6 months ‚Äì 2 years)
  • ‚úì Lower cost than university
  • ‚úì FREE at government VTA centers

üåç International Recognition

  • ‚úì Sri Lanka (government & private employers)
  • ‚úì Middle East ‚Äì GCC countries (priority NVQ workers)
  • ‚úì UK (aligns with UK qualification framework)
  • ‚úì Europe (EU countries recognize TVEC NVQ)
  • ‚úì Australia, Canada, New Zealand
  • ‚úì Singapore, Malaysia
  • ‚úì 180+ countries total
